Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for homeowners and property managers.

How do I know if I need a repair or a replacement?

We look at roof age, the location and cause of leaks, shingle condition, and whether problems are isolated or widespread. Many issues can be repaired, but some roofs are at end-of-life.

Do you offer emergency tarping?

Yes. After severe weather, tarping can help limit water intrusion until permanent repairs can be completed.

How fast can I get an estimate?

Typically within 24–48 hours depending on weather and schedule.

What areas do you serve?

Coastal Georgia and surrounding communities.
