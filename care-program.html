What is CARE?

The Comprehensive Assessment & Record Evaluation (CARE) Program is our free quarterly inspection service designed to give you peace of mind.

How It Works

  • Quarterly Inspections: We inspect your roof and exterior four times a year.
  • Pre‑ & Post‑Storm Documentation: Special visits before and after major storms help identify damage and keep your roof in top shape.
  • Detailed Reports: Receive photographs, condition notes and maintenance recommendations after each inspection.
  • Ongoing Records: Over time you’ll build a quarterly record of your roof’s condition to establish a baseline and support insurance claims.

Why It’s Beneficial

Our CARE Program offers numerous advantages for property owners:

  • Peace of Mind: Know the exact condition of your roof year‑round and catch issues before they become major problems.
  • Early Damage Detection: Prevent costly repairs by addressing minor wear and storm damage early.
  • Insurance Documentation: Maintain proof of maintenance and damage comparisons to streamline claims.
  • Faster Claims: Comprehensive before‑and‑after reports make insurance assessments easier and could lead to quicker payouts.
  • Protect Your Investment: Extend the lifespan of your roof and enhance your property’s value through consistent maintenance.
